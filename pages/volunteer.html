Beach & Coastal Cleanups : Hands-On Impact

Our most direct action against ocean pollution! Join us for regular beach and coastal cleanups where you'll help remove harmful plastic, debris, and other litter from our precious shorelines. This is a tangible way to protect marine life, beautify our beaches, and prevent pollutants from entering the ocean. All ages are welcome, and it's a great way to meet fellow ocean lovers while making an immediate, visible difference.

Education & Outreach: Spreading the Message

Be a voice for the ocean! Our education and outreach volunteers play a crucial role in raising awareness about ocean pollution and promoting sustainable practices within the community. This might involve giving presentations at schools, staffing information booths at local events, or helping to create educational materials. By sharing knowledge and inspiring others, you'll empower more people to become part of the solution and foster a lasting change in attitudes towards our marine environment.

Citizen Science: Data for Change

Help scientists gather crucial data! As a citizen science volunteer, you’ll collect and report data about marine species, water quality, and plastic waste. This info helps researchers monitor ocean health and track pollution trends. No science degree needed—just your curiosity and commitment to the ocean!

Social Media Advocacy: Make Waves Online

Love creating content? Use your digital voice to raise awareness. Share facts, design infographics, or film short reels to spotlight ocean issues. By engaging online audiences, you help spark action and build a wider community that cares about protecting life below water.
